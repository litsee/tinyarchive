New Mexico - 2015 You Are Here Series

Brand: Starbucks
Series: You Are Here Series
Material: Ceramic
Dimensions: 5.7 in. W x 3.8 in. H
Capacity: 14 fl. oz.
Year Made: 2015

Description: A short white mug with a pueblo landscape wrapping around the sides. The design is made in orange, blue, and tan, depicting a pueblo with hot air balloons flying overhead. A bright orange sun floats over a blue roadrunner next to the building. The inside of the mug is blue.
